About CubiCasa

Revolutionizing real estate with AI-powered floor plans.

Our Story

CubiCasa was born around a simple question: Can you create a floor plan from an indoor scan? After extensive market validation and technical R&D, the answer was a resounding "Yes!" At its core, CubiCasa is an AI company, developing proprietary deep tech that powers our industry-leading floor plan solutions.

Our Mission

A floor plan on every listing! In the U.S., only 10-15% of real estate listings include a floor plan, while in countries like Australia and the Nordics, they are standard. We aim to bridge this gap and make real estate transactions more transparent and efficient.
